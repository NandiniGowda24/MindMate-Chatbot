Mental Health Self-Assessment Quiz

1. In the last 2 weeks, have you often been bothered by little interest or pleasure in doing things you usually enjoy?

2. In the last month, have you had thoughts of killing yourself, or wished you were dead?

3. In the last 2 weeks, have you felt nervous, anxious, or on edge nearly every day, and found it hard to control the worry?

4. Have you ever had a period lasting at least 4 days where you felt unusually energetic, needed less sleep, talked much more than usual, and had racing thoughts?

5. Have you ever heard voices that others could not hear, or felt people were plotting to harm you, when others said this was not true?

6. Have you ever experienced a traumatic event, and since then had repeated distressing memories, nightmares, or felt like you were reliving it?

7. Do you often have unwanted thoughts that keep coming back (like contamination, harm, or symmetry), and do you feel driven to perform certain actions repeatedly to reduce distress?
